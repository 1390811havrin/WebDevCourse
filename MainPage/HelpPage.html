FAQ

Food tracks how much surplus food supplies your population is currently stockpiling. It directly effects growth rate. Larger Amounts of food will result in greater population growth. Too little food and your people will starve and die.

Gold is derived from buildings and your population at the end of every month. More Gold means you can build more. More buildings mean more gold. All around a good thing to have.

Reknown is derived from how large your population is and certain buildings. Higher reknown means that your city is more well known and increases the likelihood of events good or bad.

Security decreases the chances of an event happening as well as increasing the likelihood of an good event occurring if an event happens to occur. It is derived from buildings.

Storage acts to limit how much food you can stockpile. You cannot stockpile more food than you have room to store it. Storage is derived from buildings.

Housing acts to limit how large your population may grow. If your population grows to large without housing your moral will decrease and you will have lower tax income and population growth.

Eventually will be implemented to effect certain crafts.
